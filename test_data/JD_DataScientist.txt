Job Title: Data Scientist

Description:
We are seeking a talented Data Scientist to derive insights from our vast datasets. You will work closely with product and engineering teams to build machine learning models that power our recommendation engine.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and implement machine learning models for recommendation systems.
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns.
- Build data pipelines to clean and process data.
- Communicate findings to stakeholders through visualizations and reports.

Qualifications:
- Masters or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.
- 3+ years of experience in Data Science or Machine Learning.
- Proficiency in Python and libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn.
- Experience with Deep Learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch).
- Strong SQL skills for data extraction.
- Experience with Big Data tools (Spark, Hadoop) is desirable.
